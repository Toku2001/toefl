## 原文
Painting techniques have developed and changed significantly throughout different historical periods. Early artists often used natural `pigments` on `cave` walls, creating simple shapes and figures. `As time went on`, methods became more `advanced`, with the use of oil paints and canvas in the Renaissance allowing for detailed images and `lifelike` `portraits`. Later movements, such as Impressionism, introduced new approaches that favored light and color `over fine detail`. These `evolving` techniques reflect changing ideas about art and creativity `across history`.

## 記号付き読解

### 1.
[S] Painting techniques [V] have developed and changed [adv.] significantly [prep.] throughout [M] different historical periods.

- S: Painting techniques
- V: have developed and changed
- M: significantly, throughout different historical periods

**構成・読み方ポイント**
- 現在完了形（have developed and changed）で「過去から現在までの変化」を示す文。
- significantly は動詞句を強める副詞で、throughout ... は期間を示す前置詞句。
- TOEFLでは「時代全体の流れ」を問う設問につながりやすい導入文。

### 2.
[S] Early artists [adv.] often [V] used [O] natural pigments [prep.] on [M] cave walls, [Ving] creating [O] simple shapes and figures.

- S: Early artists
- V: used
- O: natural pigments
- M: often, on cave walls
- Ving: creating simple shapes and figures（結果・付帯状況）

**構成・読み方ポイント**
- 主節（Early artists ... used ...）に分詞構文（creating ...）が追加された形。
- creating は「どのような結果になったか」を補足し、前文の内容を具体化する。
- on cave walls は場所の特定。手段（natural pigments）と場所を切り分けて読むと理解しやすい。

### 3.
[conj.] As [S] time [V] went on, [S] methods [V] became [C] more advanced, [prep.] with [O] the use of oil paints and canvas in the Renaissance [Ving] allowing [prep.] for [O] detailed images and lifelike portraits.

- As time went on: 時を表す従属節
- S: methods
- V: became
- C: more advanced
- with + 名詞 + Ving: 付帯状況（油彩とキャンバスの使用が可能にして）

**構成・読み方ポイント**
- 文頭の As time went on は時間の推移を示す従属節で、主節の変化（became more advanced）を導く。
- became + C（more advanced）で「状態変化」の型。
- with the use of ... allowing for ... は付帯状況の長い修飾で、技術進歩の具体的中身を説明している。

### 4.
[S] Later movements, [M] such as Impressionism, [V] introduced [O] new approaches [rel.] that [V] favored [O] light and color [prep.] over [O] fine detail.

- S: Later movements
- M: such as Impressionism（挿入）
- V: introduced
- O: new approaches
- rel.: that favored ...（approaches を修飾）

**構成・読み方ポイント**
- such as Impressionism は挿入情報なので、主幹は Later movements introduced new approaches。
- that favored ... は関係代名詞節で approaches の内容説明。
- over fine detail は比較の前置詞で「細部よりも」を表し、対比（light/color vs. fine detail）を作る。

### 5.
[S] These evolving techniques [V] reflect [O] changing ideas [prep.] about [O] art and creativity [prep.] across [M] history.

- S: These evolving techniques
- V: reflect
- O: changing ideas
- M: about art and creativity, across history

**構成・読み方ポイント**
- reflect は「A reflects B（AはBを反映する）」の頻出学術表現。
- changing ideas が中心名詞句で、about ... がその内容を限定。
- across history は文全体のスコープを広げ、段落のまとめ文として機能する。

## 和訳
1. 絵画技法は、さまざまな歴史時代を通じて大きく発展し、変化してきた。
2. 初期の芸術家たちはしばしば洞窟の壁に天然顔料を使い、単純な形や人物像を描いた。
3. 時が経つにつれて技法はより高度になり、ルネサンス期には油絵具とキャンバスの使用によって、細密な画像や写実的な肖像を描くことが可能になった。
4. その後、印象派のような運動は、細部よりも光と色を重視する新しい手法を導入した。
5. こうした進化する技法は、歴史を通じて芸術と創造性に対する考え方が変化してきたことを反映している。
