# テンプレート作成

## 方針

- [SantaAI/Email/template.md](SantaAI/Email/template.md) と同じく、どの設問にも使える差し替え式にする
- Discussion問題で必要な要素を固定化する
- まず立場を明確にし、理由を2つ示し、反対意見への短い反論で締める

## 満点回答の構成フロー

1. 自分の立場を1文で明示する
2. 理由1を述べ、具体的な効果を説明する
3. 理由2を述べ、運用面・長期面の利点を示す
4. 反対意見の弱点を1-2文で指摘する
5. 立場を再確認して締める

## 使い回せる汎用フレーズ

### 1) 立場表明
- I believe that [position].
- In my view, [position] is the better approach.
- I strongly support [option A/B] for two main reasons.

### 2) 理由1（効果・メリット）
- First, [option] helps [organization/people] to [benefit].
- This allows [organization] to [positive result].
- As a result, [clear advantage].

### 3) 理由2（運用・実行可能性）
- Second, [option] makes it easier to [manage/plan/allocate resources].
- It also improves [efficiency/fairness/consistency].
- Over time, this leads to [long-term benefit].

### 4) 反対意見への対応
- Some people argue that [opposing view].
- However, this can cause [problem].
- Therefore, [your option] remains more practical and effective.

### 5) 結論
- For these reasons, I believe [position].
- Overall, [option] is the best choice for [context].

## 問題例（寄付金の使い道）への当てはめ例

### 問い

Charitable organizations are organizations that help people in need or those that advance particular causes. Charitable organizations are funded by donors, people who donate money to the organization because they believe in its mission. Charitable organizations often deal with the following issue: should donors be allowed to decide how the money they donate will be used? Or should the charitable organization have complete control over how the donated funds will be spent? Which approach do you think is best for an organization? Why?

### Aさんの意見

It's in an organization's best interest to allow donors to choose how their money is spent. It helps organizations to find out which programs or services their supporters care most about. Based on this knowledge, the organization can prioritize activities that attract the most donors.

### Bさんの意見

I believe that organizations should always have power over how their funds are allocated. After all, donors already have a choice about which charity or organization to donate to. It would be too confusing for an organization to have to manage funds according to donor preferences instead of focusing on its core mission.

### 模範回答例（Bさん寄り）

I believe that charitable organizations should have full control over how donated funds are spent.

First, professional staff members understand urgent needs better than individual donors. This allows organizations to allocate money where it is most needed, such as emergency food, medicine, or shelter.

Second, centralized decision-making makes budgeting more efficient and consistent. If every donor set different conditions, organizations would spend too much time managing restrictions instead of helping people.

Some people argue that donor choice increases motivation to donate. However, donors can already choose which organization to support, so giving them full control after donating is unnecessary.

For these reasons, I believe organizations should decide how donations are used.

## 書くときのポイント

- 最初の1文で立場を明確にする
- 理由は2つに絞り、各理由で「何が良いか」を具体化する
- 反対意見は要約してから短く反論する
- 結論で立場を言い直して一貫性を示す
- 文字数が限られる場合は、例示を1つに絞って論理の明確さを優先する
