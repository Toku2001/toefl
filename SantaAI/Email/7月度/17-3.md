# 問題文
```
Write an email to the Service Department. In your email, do the following:
- Describe the technical issue with your headphones.
- Explain how the problem affects your listening experience.
- Request repair or replacement options.
Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.
You visited an electronics store last weekend and bought a new pair of headphones.
After using them for a few days, you discovered that the sound often cuts out on one side. You would like to contact the store's Service Department for help.
```

# 評価と修正案

## 主な問題点

| 箇所 | 現在 | 問題 | 修正 |
|------|------|------|------|
| "cuts our on one side" | cuts our | タイプミス | "cuts out" |
| "not conforable" | conforable | タイプミス | "comfortable" |
| "listen music" | listen music | 前置詞の誤り | "listen to music" |
| "Could you send a message how..." | 文法エラー | 不自然な表現 | "Could you please provide repair or replacement options?" |
| "If you are possible" | 文法エラー | 主語の誤り | 削除（冗長） |
| "I would like to contact..." | 重複 | すでに連絡済みのため不要 | "I would appreciate your assistance." |

## 改善版例

```
Dear Service Department,

I am writing to report an issue with a new pair of headphones that I purchased last weekend. 
After using them for a few days, I discovered that the sound often cuts out on one side.

This problem significantly affects my listening experience, as I cannot enjoy music or radio 
programming properly with only one side functioning. This is very inconvenient for me.

Could you please provide information about repair or replacement options? 
I would appreciate your prompt assistance in resolving this matter.

Sincerely,
Toku
```

## スコア改善ポイント

1. **文法エラーの排除**
   - タイプミス（cuts our → cuts out, conforable → comfortable）
   - 前置詞の修正（listen music → listen to music）
   - 冗長な表現を削除（"If you are possible, I would like to contact..." は不自然）

2. **3要件の明確な構成**
   - 段落1（理由）: 購入時期と技術的問題を簡潔に説明
   - 段落2（懸念）: 音声の片側障害が聴取体験に与える影響を詳述
   - 段落3（依頼）: 修理・交換オプションについての明確なリクエスト

3. **敬語・正式表現の統一**
   - "Could you send a message" → "Could you please provide information"
   - "Best Regards" → "Sincerely," （より標準的）
   - "I would appreciate your prompt assistance" で丁寧さを強調

## 推奨される汎用文法表現（復習）

- **問題報告の開始**: "I am writing to report an issue with..."
- **問題の影響**: "This problem [significantly/greatly] affects my [listening experience]."
- **明確な依頼**: "Could you please provide information about [repair/replacement options]?"
- **感謝の表現**: "I would appreciate your prompt assistance in resolving this matter."
- **締めくくり**: "Sincerely," / "Thank you for your attention to this matter."
