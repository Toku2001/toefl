# 問題文
```
Write an email to the Registrar's Office. In your email, do the following:
- Describe the conflict between your class schedule and your job.
- Explain why it is important for you to resolve this issue.
- Request information about possible solutions or alternative classes.
Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.
You are a university student who registered for a statistics class, but the class schedule has recently changed and now conflicts with your part-time job. You need to contact the registrar's office for help with your enrollment options.
```

# 私の回答
```
Dear Registar's Office
I am writing to report an issue with my class schedule and my part-time job.
The class schedule has recently changed and now conflicts with my part-time job, so I won't be able to attend my class.
If I miss the class, my grade may go down, so this issue is very important for me to resolve.
Could you please let me know if there are any possible solutions or alternative classes available?
```

# 評価と修正案

## 主な問題点

| 箇所 | 現在 | 問題 | 修正 |
|------|------|------|------|
| "Dear Registar's Office" | Registar's | タイプミス | "Dear Registrar's Office" |
| "join my class" | join | 不自然な表現 | "attend my class" |
| "If I will miss" | will miss | 条件文の形が不自然 | "If I miss" |
| "be grade down of my review" | 文法エラー | 意味が伝わりにくい | "my grade may go down" |
| "Please let me know how I can do it" | how I can do it | 依頼が曖昧 | "Could you please let me know if there are any possible solutions?" |

## TOEFL メール高スコア向け推奨構成

**3要件の明確な分離：「理由 → 懸念 → 依頼」**

```
段落1（理由）: 問題の簡潔な説明
	"I am writing to report an issue with..."

段落2（懸念）: 重要性や困る点を説明
	"This issue is important because..."

段落3（依頼）: 明確なリクエスト
	"Could you please let me know if there are any possible solutions or alternative classes?"
```

## 改善版例

```
Dear Registrar's Office,

I am writing to report a problem with my statistics class schedule. The class time has recently changed, and now it conflicts with my part-time job.

This issue is very important for me to resolve because I need my job to pay for my school expenses. If I miss the class, my grade may go down.

Could you please let me know if there are any possible solutions or alternative classes available? I would appreciate any help you can offer.

Sincerely,
Toku
```

## スコア改善ポイント（4点→6点への到達）

1. **文法エラーを減らす**
	 - "If I miss" を使って自然な条件文にする
	 - "be grade down" のような不自然な表現を避ける

2. **内容を3段落で整理する**
	 - 問題説明
	 - 重要性の説明
	 - 解決策の依頼

3. **正式なメール表現を使う**
	 - "attend my class" を使う
	 - "Could you please let me know" で丁寧さを出す

## 推奨される汎用文法表現

- **問題報告の開始**: "I am writing to report an issue with..."
- **重要性の説明**: "This issue is important because..."
- **条件文**: "If I miss the class, my grade may go down."
- **丁寧な依頼**: "Could you please let me know if there are any possible solutions?"
- **締めくくり**: "I would appreciate any help you can offer."
