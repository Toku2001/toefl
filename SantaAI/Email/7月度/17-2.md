# 問題文
```
Write an email to Customer Support. In your email, do the following:
- Describe the issue with your recent sneaker order.
- Explain how the mistake is causing inconvenience to you.
- Request a replacement with the correct size and color.
Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.
You ordered a pair of sneakers from an online retailer last week. When the package arrived yesterday, you noticed that you received the wrong size and color. You want to resolve this issue with the retailer.
```

# 評価と修正案

## 主な問題点

| 箇所 | 現在 | 問題 | 修正 |
|------|------|------|------|
| "Hi, I am Toku." | Hi, I am Toku. | 自己紹介は不要 | 削除 |
| "yestereday" | yestereday | タイプミス | "yesterday" |
| "I ordered its size is..." | 文法エラー | 主語と述語の不一致 | "I ordered sneakers in size 28.0 and black color" |
| "recieved" | recieved | タイプミス | "received" |
| "how I can replacement it" | replacement | 名詞の誤用 | "how I can replace it" または "for a replacement" |
| "Could you please let me know how..." | 弱い表現 | より直接的な依頼が良い | "Could you please send me" |
| "If it is possible, please let me know." | 重複 | 前の文と重複 | 削除 |

## 改善版例

```
Dear Customer Support,

I am writing to report an issue with a pair of sneakers I ordered last week. 
When the package arrived yesterday, I noticed that I received the wrong size and color.

I ordered sneakers in size 28.0 with black color, but I received size 30.0 in red instead. 
This mistake is causing me considerable inconvenience, as I cannot use these sneakers 
as intended.

Could you please send me a replacement with the correct size (28.0) and color (black)? 
I would appreciate your prompt assistance in resolving this matter.

Sincerely,
Toku
```

## スコア改善ポイント

1. **文法エラーの排除**
   - タイプミス（yestereday → yesterday, recieved → received）
   - 文構造の修正（"I ordered its size is..." → "I ordered sneakers in..."）

2. **3要件の明確な構成**
   - 段落1（理由）: 注文内容と受け取った問題を簡潔に説明
   - 段落2（懸念）: 具体的な相違点と不便さを詳述
   - 段落3（依頼）: 明確な交換リクエスト

3. **不要な重複を削除**
   - "let me know" の重複表現をシンプルにする
   - "If it is possible" は削除（丁寧さは "Could you please" で表現）

## 推奨される汎用文法表現（復習）

- **具体的な詳細の説明**: "I ordered [item] in [size/color], but I received [different specification]."
- **不便さの表現**: "This mistake is causing me considerable inconvenience."
- **明確な依頼**: "Could you please send me a replacement with the correct [specification]?"
- **感謝の表現**: "I would appreciate your prompt assistance in resolving this matter."
