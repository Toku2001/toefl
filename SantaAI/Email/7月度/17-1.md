# 問題文
```
Write an email to TechWorld Electronics. In your email, do the following:
- Describe the issue you are experiencing with the wireless mouse.
- Explain the steps you have already taken to try to fix it.
- Request instructions for repair or information about a replacement.
Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.
You recently purchased a new wireless mouse from TechWorld Electronics, but after a few days, it stopped working. You have tried changing the batteries and troubleshooting the device, but nothing has worked, so you want to contact their customer support.
```

# 評価と修正案

## 主な問題点

| 箇所 | 現在 | 問題 | 修正 |
|------|------|------|------|
| "Hi, 1 am Toku." | 1 am | タイプミス＆自己紹介は不要 | 削除するか "I am Toku" |
| "daysm" | daysm | タイプミス | "days" |
| "because...but..." | 1文が長すぎる | 2つの独立句を接続詞で無理につなぐ | ピリオドで2文に分割 |
| "so I want" | 新しい文で開始 | 前文との接続が弱い | ", so I want..." で前文と接続 |
| "I hopw you that make" | hopw＆文法エラー | タイプミス＋不自然な表現 | "I hope you can repair or replace" |
| "exchange new one" | 冠詞不足 | "a new one" に修正 |

## TOEFL メール高スコア向け推奨構成

**3要件の明確な分離：「理由 → 懸念 → 依頼」**

```
段落1（理由）: 問題の簡潔な説明
  "I am writing to report an issue with..."

段落2（懸念）: 具体的な対応状況
  "I have already tried [action], but [result]."

段落3（依頼）: 明確なリクエスト
  "Could you please provide repair instructions or information 
   about a replacement?"
```

## 改善版例

```
Dear TechWorld Electronics,

I am writing to report an issue with a wireless mouse I recently purchased 
from your company. After a few days of use, the device stopped working entirely.

I have attempted to resolve this problem by changing the batteries and 
troubleshooting the device, but unfortunately, nothing has worked.

Could you please provide repair instructions or information about a 
replacement? I would appreciate any assistance you can offer.

Sincerely,
Toku
```

## スコア改善ポイント（4点→6点への到達）

1. **文法エラーの完全排除**
   - タイプミス（daysm, hopw, 1 am）を確認
   - 接続詞の正しい使用方法（"because...but..."は避ける）

2. **構成の明確化**
   - 3要件を異なる段落に分離
   - 各段落の目的を明確にする

3. **敬語・正式表現の統一**
   - "hope" より "Could you please" の方がより丁寧
   - "I want to contact" より "I am writing to report" の方が正式

## 推奨される汎用文法表現

- **問題報告の開始**: "I am writing to report an issue/problem with..."
- **対応済み状況**: "I have already tried/attempted [action], but [result]."
- **丁寧なリクエスト**: "Could you please provide information about...?"
- **感謝の表現**: "I would appreciate any assistance you can offer."
- **締めくくり**: "Thank you for your prompt response." / "I look forward to hearing from you."
