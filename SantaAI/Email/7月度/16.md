- Describe your concerns with your group member's lack of participation.
- Explain how this situation is impacting your group's progress.

Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.
You are a university student who has been assigned to work on a group project in Professor Lee's class. However, one of your group members has not been participating or submitting their part of the work, and you are worried about the final grade.

# Copilot講評

## 総評
内容の方向性は正しく、課題の3要件に触れられています。ただし、文法の正確さとメール形式の自然さが不足しており、3点評価になりやすい答案です。英語の基本ミスを減らし、依頼文を明確化すれば4-5点に上げやすいです。

## 良かった点
- 課題の中心問題（メンバー不参加）を明確に書けている。
- グループ進行と成績への不安に触れており、2要件目に対応できている。
- 教授に助言を求める意図があり、3要件目の方向性がある。

## 改善すべき点
- 宛名と書き出しを自然にする（Dear Lee -> Dear Professor Lee,）。
- 文法ミスを減らす（lam -> I am, has problem -> has a problem, thier -> their など）。
- 不自然な構文を直す（I worried about we will... -> I am worried that we will...）。
- 依頼文を明確にする（how I suggest our member ではなく、how to handle this issue）。
- 具体性を1文追加する（例: 連絡回数、どの作業が止まっているか）。

## 改善例（短め完成版）
```text
Dear Professor Lee,
I am writing because one member of my group has not been participating or submitting their part of the project.
This is affecting our progress because we cannot complete the next steps without that part. I am worried that this situation will hurt our final grade.
Could you please advise us on how to handle this issue? If possible, I would appreciate your guidance on how to divide responsibilities more clearly.
Thank you for your time.
Sincerely,
Toku
```

## 汎用テンプレート
- 理由: I am writing to report a problem with our group project.
- 懸念・影響: One member has not submitted their work, and this is delaying our progress.
- 成績への影響: We are concerned that this may affect our final grade.
- 依頼: Could you please advise us on how to handle this situation?
- 結び: Thank you for your time and guidance.

## 6点に近づくチェックリスト
1. 課題の3要件（理由・影響・依頼）を1つずつ明示したか。
2. 文法ミスが頻出していないか（主語+動詞、冠詞、スペル）。
3. 依頼文が具体的か（advice, next steps, possible meeting）。
4. 丁寧なメール形式か（Professor, Thank you, Sincerely）。
