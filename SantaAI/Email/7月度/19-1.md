# 問題文
```
Write an email to Jason. In your email, do the following:
- Describe the current status of the project.
- Explain how the delays could impact the final deadline.
- Suggest ways to speed up the remaining tasks.
Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.
You have been collaborating on a group project at work with your colleague, Jason.
Recently, you noticed that several tasks are behind schedule, which may delay the project's completion. You want to address these concerns with Jason.
```

# 私の回答
```
Dear Jason
I am writing to report an issue with our project's several tasks because our project may delay the project's completion.
If this tasks don't complete, our grade will be down by class teacher.
So, I want to talk about this problem with our team mate but I don't know address about eveyone in our team mate.
Could you tell me about address about our team mate?
It you know, please let me know.
```

# 要望
- ``模範回答`` に6点満点の回答を記載してください
- ``解説`` に``私の回答の良い点・悪かった点を記載してください

# 模範回答
```
Dear Jason,

I wanted to update you on the current status of our project. Although we have already completed the research phase and finished most of the presentation slides, several important tasks are still behind schedule. In particular, the data analysis section has not been finalized yet, and the visual materials for the final report are still incomplete.

If these delays continue, we may not be able to finish the entire project by the final deadline. That could leave us with less time to review our work, correct mistakes, and prepare for the presentation. As a result, the overall quality of the project could suffer.

To speed up the remaining tasks, I suggest that we divide the unfinished work more clearly among our team members and set short deadlines for each part. We could also have a brief meeting tomorrow to decide who will handle the data analysis, editing, and visual design. If necessary, we might ask another teammate to help with the report so that we can stay on schedule.

Please let me know what you think.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

# 解説
## 私の回答の良い点
- Jasonに対して丁寧に連絡しようとしており、メールの基本的な形を意識できています。
- プロジェクトの遅れについて相談したい、という中心テーマは伝わります。
- 相手に協力を求める文で終えており、メールとしての目的はあります。

## 私の回答の悪かった点
- 設問で求められている3点のうち、現在の進捗状況の説明と、遅れを取り戻すための具体策が十分に書かれていません。
- 「our grade will be down by class teacher」のように、問題文は職場のグループプロジェクトなのに、学校の課題のような内容になっており、場面設定とずれています。
- 文法ミスがいくつかあります。たとえば「If this tasks don't complete」は「If these tasks are not completed」や「If these tasks are delayed」のように直すと自然です。
- 「address about our team mate」の表現は不自然です。言いたいことが「連絡先」なら「contact information for our teammates」や「their email addresses」が適切です。
- 「It you know, please let me know.」は「If you know, please let me know.」ですが、やや不自然なので「Could you let me know their email addresses?」のほうが自然です。
- 全体として、同じ語の繰り返しが多く、「project may delay the project's completion」のように不自然な表現があります。簡潔に「several tasks are behind schedule, which may delay the project」などとするとよくなります。

## 改善のポイント
- まず現在の状況を書く。
- 次に、その遅れが締切にどう影響するかを書く。
- 最後に、役割分担や短いミーティングなど、具体的な改善策を書く。
- 職場の同僚へのメールとして、自然で実務的な表現を使う。
